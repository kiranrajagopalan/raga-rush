# Image Prompts for Raga Rush Video

**Master video format:** 16:9 landscape (1920x1080)
**Image generation tool:** Nano Banana 2 (via Gemini)
**Thinking mode:** High — for better composition and detail accuracy

---

## Style Direction

All images share one visual language: **warm analog photography with visible texture and imperfection.** Muted warm tones (ochre, amber, gold, cream), shallow depth of field, soft grain, natural daylight. Nothing glossy, nothing hyper-sharp. The cohesion comes from consistent warmth, f/2.8 shallow focus, and natural imperfections across every image.

## Nano Banana 2 Tips

- **Set thinking to High** for all prompts — these are compositionally complex
- **Generate at 2K or 4K** and downscale for the video — gives cleaner grain texture
- **Iterate conversationally**: if the first result is close, say "Keep the composition but increase the film grain" or "Same image but warmer, more amber tones" rather than re-prompting from scratch
- **Palette consistency across 3, 4, 5, 6**: After generating Image 3, reference it when prompting 4 and 5 — "Same paper texture and overhead angle as the previous image, but with a cool palette of five swatches"
- **If results look too AI-clean**: Add "more visible imperfections — paper fiber texture, uneven paint edges, slight dust specks, natural film grain"
- **For the B&W monkey (Image 7)**: If it comes out too smooth, iterate with "higher contrast, heavier grain, more motion blur on the limbs"
- **Aspect ratio**: Specify it in the prompt AND in the Gemini generation settings for best results
